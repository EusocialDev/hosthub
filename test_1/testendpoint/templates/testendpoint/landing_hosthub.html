What is HostHub?

A central hub for restaurant communication and action.

Restaurants lose revenue every day from missed calls, unanswered questions, delayed callbacks, forgotten voicemails, and untracked guest requests. HostHub was built to solve that operational gap.

Built for real hospitality operations

HostHub is not just a dashboard. It is a workflow layer for restaurants that need better control over incoming guest communication, especially when the team is busy during service.

Works with Voice AI agents

HostHub integrates with Voice AI agents that can answer calls, take reservation requests, handle carryout orders, answer common questions, capture voicemail, and route conversations into clear actionable items for staff.

Why it matters

Restaurants do not just lose calls. They lose revenue.

A missed phone call can mean a lost table, a lost order, a lost private event, or a guest who never comes back. HostHub helps restaurants reduce that loss by making sure inbound demand is captured, categorized, and visible.

Reservations

Capture reservation calls even when staff cannot answer in the moment, helping restaurants avoid empty tables caused by missed opportunities.

Carryout

Keep takeout demand from slipping away by handling carryout calls and organizing order details in a more structured workflow.

Guest experience

Provide faster, more consistent answers to guest questions while reducing pressure on the in-house team during peak hours.
